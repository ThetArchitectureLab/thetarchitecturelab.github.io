RESIDENCE FOR MR.A
Area: 653 sqmt.
Year of Completion: 2025
In a world characterized by excess and noise, the allure of minimalism shines through in the form of a 3 BHK residence that captures the essence of elegant simplicity. This minimalist haven, with its clean lines, uncluttered spaces, and a deliberate absence of unnecessary adornments, demonstrates that a true home is one where function and form coexist harmoniously.
The heart of this 3 BHK residence is the art of space utilization. Each room is a testament to the idea that less can indeed be more. The living room, bathed in soft, diffused natural light, features a carefully curated selection of furniture that adheres to the principles of minimalism. Clean, straight lines and muted colours create an ambiance of tranquillity, inviting inhabitants and guests to unwind in an environment free from distractions.
