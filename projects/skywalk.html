SKYWALK IN PROMENADE BEACH
Area: 1702.26 sqmt.
Year of Completion: 2028
The pondicherry pier partially collapsed in 2022 due to high waves as a result of deep depression over the bay of bengal.
Using this fact, instead of reconstructing another bridge, a sky-walk can be constructed at the same spot, which can act as a view point.
It'll provide a wider view of the promenade beach and also a view of the white town of pondicherry, showing off the beautiful french architecture incorporated in india.
CONCEPT
Since the site is located in the french colony of pondicherry, The design of the sky-walk will incorporate the elements of the french architecture in pondicherry.
Some of the elements from french architecture considered for this design are:
  • Columns
  • Arches
  • Stained glass
