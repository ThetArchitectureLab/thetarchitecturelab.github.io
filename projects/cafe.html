CAFE DESIGNED USING RAMMED EARTH
Area: 2400 sqft.
Year of Completion: 2023
The café's structure, crafted from rammed earth, celebrates the simplicity and durability of this ancient building method. The walls, handcrafted from layers of compressed earth, showcase a rich tapestry of natural hues, reminiscent of the earth's diverse landscapes. The textured walls, basking in sunlight, exude a sense of grounded tranquillity, inviting guests into a space that seamlessly merges with its environment.
Inside the café, the ambience is a blend of organic textures and cozy minimalism. Wooden furniture, crafted from reclaimed materials, complements the earthy walls, offering patrons a serene and inviting space to savor their culinary experience. Soft lighting accents the natural tones, creating an ambiance that encourages relaxation and unhurried enjoyment.
