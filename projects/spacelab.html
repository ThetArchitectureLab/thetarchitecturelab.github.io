SPACE LAB
Area: 1607 sqmt.
Year of Completion: 2029
Futurism was a movement started in the early 20th century and the key focus of was to represent a dynamic vision of the future. Science fiction has used this movement to create some of the greatest pieces of media of all time, often set in outer space. Space travel has fascinated mankind for centuries and technology has made it possible to experience the life of an astronaut light years away from planet earth.
What better way to elevate a new-age dining experience than to be aboard a spaceship, hurtling amidst the stars? The Space Laboratory, the first of its kind in Bangalore, is a restaurant that invites you onboard for a (g)astronomical experience. The ship offers its passengers a view into the wonders of outer space with galaxies, comets, and constellations right outside the windows.
CONCEPT
The concept of a journey through space is solidified with the outdoor space created to resemble a different planet. Diners feel like they are transported to a new world as they step out of the ship, abundant in technological equipment, and into an otherworldly exterior.
Augmented reality heightens the experience with holograms and altered reality to replicate space details and give customers the most authentic experience possible.
